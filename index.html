About Me

Hey — I'm Aflah. A Full Stack Developer from Kerala who genuinely enjoys the craft of building software. Not just shipping features, but writing code that actually makes sense six months later.

I studied B.Com with Computer Applications at NAHER Arts & Science College, then went deep into modern full-stack development through TALROP's intensive program. That combination — business thinking + technical depth — is how I approach every project.

Right now I'm at Yuai Soft Solutions building production-level frontend components and backend services in an Agile environment. Before that, I shipped a complete desktop time-tracking app (TimeLogger) for a freelance client — from zero to deployed, solo.

My philosophy: keep it simple, keep it clean, ship it working. Fancy architecture that nobody can maintain is a bug, not a feature.
