I'm a Full Stack Developer from Kerala, India, specializing in React, Django REST Framework, and PostgreSQL. Beyond my professional work, I've built a desktop application using ElectronJS and developed several self-initiated full-stack projects to continuously sharpen my skills. I hold a Degree,B.Com in Computer Applications and completed intensive full-stack training with TALROP. I'm passionate about building reliable software that solves real business problems and creates measurable impact.
